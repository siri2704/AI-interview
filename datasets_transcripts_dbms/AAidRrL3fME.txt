The Entity relationship ER model is a conceptual data model used in database Management Systems dbms to represent and design databases it visually depicts entities attributes and relationships between entities key points about the ER model include entities represent Real World objects such as customers products or employees each entity has attributes that describe its properties attributes characteristics or properties of an entity for example a customer entity may have attributes like name address and email relationships connections between entities showing how they are related relationships can be one-to-one one-to-many or many to many cardinality describes the number of instances in a relationship such as one to one or one-to-many
